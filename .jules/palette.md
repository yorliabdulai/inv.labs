## 2026-06-06 - Add aria-labels to OnboardingTour icon buttons
**Learning:** Interactive icon-only control elements in dynamic or temporary flows (like the onboarding tour) often miss accessibility attributes. They require descriptive aria-labels for screen readers and explicit focus-visible classes for keyboard navigation.
**Action:** Always ensure all interactive icon-only control elements include descriptive aria-label attributes and focus-visible styling, even in temporary overlays or tours.
