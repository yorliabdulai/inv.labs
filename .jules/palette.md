## 2026-05-28 - Focus States and ARIA Labels in Onboarding Flows
**Learning:** Interactive elements in temporary or dynamic flows like onboarding tours often lack fundamental accessibility (ARIA labels for icons and clear keyboard focus states), making them difficult to navigate for keyboard and screen-reader users.
**Action:** Ensure all custom buttons in overlays/tours use standard 'focus-visible' utility classes and descriptive 'aria-label' attributes.
